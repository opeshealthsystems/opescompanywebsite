## The OPES Platform: What It Does

The OPES Health Systems platform is a comprehensive, integrated hospital management system covering the full range of health facility operations.

### Patient Management

Every patient has a single, unified digital record — accessible from any authorised workstation in the facility, instantly, without the delays and failures of a paper file system. Patient registration takes under three minutes for new patients. Returning patients are identified in seconds.

The patient record includes demographic information, insurance details, complete medical history, current medications, documented allergies, all previous consultation notes, all investigation results, and the full billing and payment history. No more partial pictures. No more missing history.

### Appointment Scheduling

Patients can book appointments via WhatsApp, online, or by telephone through a receptionist. Automated reminders go out 24 hours and 2 hours before each appointment, via SMS or WhatsApp. No-show rates fall by 40–60%. Morning queues become manageable.

Clinician schedules are managed in the system — no double-booking, no phantom appointments, no confusion about who is seeing which patient when.

### Clinical Records and Prescribing

Clinicians record consultation findings in structured digital notes — organized by chief complaint, history, examination, assessment, and plan. Diagnoses are coded. Prescriptions are entered digitally and transmitted instantly to the pharmacy module, with automatic drug interaction checking and allergy alerts.

Every prescription is part of the patient's complete medication history, visible at every future consultation.

### Pharmacy and Inventory Management

Every dispensing event is recorded against the patient record and inventory database simultaneously. Stock levels update in real time. Reorder alerts fire automatically when stock reaches the defined minimum. FEFO is enforced digitally. Expiry alerts fire at 90, 60, and 30 days before expiry dates.

Stockouts become exceptions rather than routine. Expired medicine waste falls by 70–90%.

### Billing and Revenue Cycle

Every service recorded in any clinical module is automatically added to the patient's running invoice. Billing is complete when clinical care is complete. CNPS claims are generated automatically in the correct format. Insurance submission is tracked. Rejected claims are flagged for rapid correction.

Revenue leakage falls from a typical 20–25% to under 3%.

### Management Dashboard

Hospital directors see real-time performance data across the entire facility: patient volumes, revenue, outstanding receivables, pharmacy stock levels, staff utilisation, and clinical indicators — all on a single dashboard, updated continuously.

Management decisions are made with data, not intuition.

### DHIS2 Reporting

Data is exportable in DHIS2-compatible formats for national health information system reporting, making compliance with government reporting requirements automatic.

## What Makes OPES Different

**Built for Cameroon.** Not adapted from another market. Built from scratch with Cameroonian realities as the design brief.

**Offline-first architecture.** The system operates from local storage when internet is unavailable. Connectivity is never a single point of failure.

**Genuinely bilingual.** Full French and English across every screen, every label, every document, every error message. Per-user language selection. Support documentation and training in both languages.

**CNPS-compatible billing.** Not "we can configure it to work with CNPS" — built in, working, and deployed in Cameroonian facilities.

**XAF denomination.** All pricing, billing, and reporting in Central African CFA francs. No currency risk. No conversion complexity.

**CEMAC-region data hosting.** Patient data from Cameroonian facilities stays in the CEMAC region. Data sovereignty is a design principle, not an option.

**Local support team.** We have people in Cameroon. French and English. Reachable during Cameroonian business hours. Able to be on site when needed.

**Transparent pricing.** One clear monthly or annual subscription. No hidden fees, no per-user escalation, no charges for features that should be included.
